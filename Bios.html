Biographies:

Charles Bukowski

is a German-born American poet. Considered to be an "underground poet", his work is shameless and raw. He died in 1994 of leukemia.

Cesar Vallejo

is a Peruvian poet. He is considered one of the most greatest "poetic innovators" of the 20th century.

He died in 1938 in Paris, France.

Franz Wright

is an Austrian-born American poet. Him and his father, James Wright are the only father/son pair to have each won a Pulitzer prize. He died in 2015 of cancer.
